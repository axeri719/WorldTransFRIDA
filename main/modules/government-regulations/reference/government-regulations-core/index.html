    Government_Regulations.forestation_policy¶

    aux Policy lever

    Units: Mha/year · Graphical function

    To determine the maximum afforestation rate per year, the distribution of all scenarios described in the AR6 report for the variable Land Cover|Forest|Afforestation and Reforestation was used as a basis Based on this distribution, the median values ​​within the 90% quantile range were calculated for each year Since the afforestation value is cumulative over time, the maximum afforestation per year was computed as the average annual rate, using 2010 and 2100 as the starting and ending years, respectively. This calculation resulted in a value of 9 Mha/year. Williams et al. (2024) estimates a potential of 215 million hectares for natural tropical forest regeneration over 30 years, which corresponds to an average annual rate of ~7 Mha/year. This aligns reasonably well with the 9 Mha/year rate derived from the AR6 report, indicating some level of consistency between the findings

    Source: https://data.ece.iiasa.ac.at/ar6/#/login?redirect=%2Fworkspaces https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-024-08106-4
