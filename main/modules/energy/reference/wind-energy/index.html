    wind_energy.sensitivity_of_renewable_floor_costs_to_grid_storage_needs¶

    aux Internal calibration parameter

    Units: dmnl · Dimensions: Run · Range: 2 – 10

    These parameters are set so that the effect of having to build grid energy storage for renewable output share on floor costs follows a behaviour where costs increase (do not decrease as fast) if grid storage has to be built alongside solar and wind when they make up a large share of output. But if solar and wind is scaled further (including implied complementary storage) costs come down again. The eventual decrease of costs is motivated by the same general idea as overall learning by doing.

    Equation: 

    4.98785416359998
