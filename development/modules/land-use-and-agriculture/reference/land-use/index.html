    Land_Use.clearing_forests_for_crops¶

    flow

    Units: Mha/Year · Dimensions: Run

    Equation: 

    MIN(Land_Use.Mature_Forest/DT,  Land_Use.new_land_for_crops*Land_Use.fraction_of_new_cropland_from_forests)

    Land_Use.clearing_grassland¶

    flow

    Units: Mha/year · Dimensions: Run

    FRIDA allows conversion of Unused Land into Agricultural Land (Edgerton 2009; Lambin and Meyfroidt 2011)

    Edgerton MD. 2009. Increasing crop productivity to meet global needs for feed, food, and fuel. Plant Physiol 149(1): 7-13. doi: 10.1104/pp.108.130195.

    Lambin EF, Meyfroidt P. 2011. Global land use change, economic globalization, and the looming land scarcity. Proceedings of the national academy of sciences 108(9): 3465-3472. https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1100480108.

    Equation: 

    MIN(Land_Use.Grassland/DT,  Land_Use.new_land_for_crops*(1-Land_Use.fraction_of_new_cropland_from_forests))

    Units: Mha/year · Dimensions: Run

    Equation: 

    (Land_Use.cropland_depleting)*(1-Land_Use.fraction_of_depleting_cropland_to_grassland)
